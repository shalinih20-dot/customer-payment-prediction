# Customer Payment Prediction
Predicting when customers will pay invoices using machine learning fundamentals

## Project Goal
Apply statistical and ML concepts to predict customer payment timing, helping optimize cash flow and working capital management.

## Business Problem
Companies send invoices with payment terms (e.g., Net 30), but customers often pay late. This creates:
- Cash flow uncertainty
- Working capital strain
- Difficulty in financial planning

**Can we predict payment timing based on customer attributes and historical behavior?**

## Concepts Applied
- **Normal Distribution**: Payment timing patterns
- **Mean & Variance**: Customer payment reliability
- **Correlation Analysis**: Factors driving payment delays
- **Probability**: Payment likelihood scoring
- **Bias-Variance Tradeoff**: Model optimization
